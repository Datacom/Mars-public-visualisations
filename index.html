Government Key Results Areas

Welfare Dependence

  • Goal 1: Reduce the number of people who have been on a working age benefit for more than 12 months.
  • Note the seasonality, with increased numbers in each December quarter. Choose a December quarter, see that "Other Main Benefit" increases. Choose "Other Main Benefit", note the ages of the recipients - this is students getting a benefit over the Christmas Holiday break.

Early Childhood Education

  • Goal 2: Increase participation in early childhood education.
  • Choose different ethnicities. Note the different rates of change in ECE uptake shown in the bar graph.

Rheumatic Fever

  • Goal 3: Increase infant immunisation rates and reduce the incidence of rheumatic fever.
  • Note: Counties Manukau at three times the incidence of anything else

Assaults on children and young people

  • Goal 4: Reduce the number of assaults on children.

School Leavers

  • Goal 5: Increase the proportion of 18-year-olds with NCEA level 2 or equivalent qualification.
  • Filter by Pasifika students and level 3 qualification to see an increase in Pasifika achievement since 2009.

Highest Qualification

  • Goal 6: Increase the proportion of 25 to 34-year-olds with advanced trade qualifications, diplomas and degrees (at level 4 or above).

GDELT

Supported by Google Ideas, the GDELT Project monitors the world's broadcast, print, and web news from nearly every corner of every country in over 100 languages and identifies the people, locations, organizations, counts, themes, sources, emotions, counts, quotes and events driving our global society every second of every day, creating a free open platform for computing on the entire world. Here we have pre-filtered the data for news with an NZ connection.

GDELT: Education

As for the GDELT visualisation, but pre-filterd for Education. This vis is a bit useless, as it tends to pick up all references to "University", and therefor all pieces where an academic has been approached for comment on their field.

GDELT: Defence

As for the GDELT visualisation, but pre-filterd for Defence related articles.

  • use the search boxes to find references to the Minister/ former ministers.
